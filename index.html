About Tesseract

Imagine a day filled with brilliant speakers, thought-provoking videos, and mind-blowing conversation. By organizing an event, you can create a unique gathering in your community that will unleash new ideas, inspires, and informs.

TESSERACT event is an ever-awaited gathering that every SJCETians plates you with their full innovativeness where live TED-like talks, workshops, and performances are shared with the accumulators. This is an event fully planned, coordinated, and synchronized by each and every IEEE member of St.Joseph's College Of Engineering and Technology, Palai. The TESSERACT has its own wealthy past history over the Josephians. Lending apart all the ripple and dribble of the pandemic the worlds stand in legs. And we are proud enough to welcome all the young innovative minds to blast our your rest and to explode. Expand your horizons of knowledge with tech talks, inspiring sessions, and innovative workshops by eminent personalities.

F.A.Q

  • Where is the nearest Railway Station/Bus Station?

    The nearest railway station is the Ettumanur Railway Station which is approximately 24 kilometers away from the Campus and the nearest Bus Station is Kottaramattom Bus Station which is approximately 9 kilometers away from the Campus

  • Will bus service be provided from the Campus?

    Yes, bus service will be provided between the campus and the Kottaramattom Bus Station
