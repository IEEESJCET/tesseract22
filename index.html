About Tesseract

TESSERACT is an ever-awaited gathering that every SJCETian plates you with their full innovativeness where live TED-like talks, workshops, and performances are shared with the accumulators. This is an event fully planned, coordinated, and synchronized by each and every IEEE member of St.Joseph's College Of Engineering and Technology, Palai. TESSERACT has its own wealthy past history over the Josephians. Lending apart all the ripple and dribble of the pandemic, we are proud enough to welcome all the young innovative minds to have a mind blowing experience, to expand your horizons of knowledge with tech talks, inspiring sessions, and innovative workshops by eminent personalities.
